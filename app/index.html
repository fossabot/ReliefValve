A tool to help manage the Steam client, and apps installed via Steam. Features are based on tricks which can be done manually, but some take more work than others.

  • Manage some settings for apps installed through the Steam client.
  • Apply a "fix" to make some games that are installed but have an update available playable in offline mode.  ?
  • Basic "category" management for apps owned on Steam.
  • Change most of the Steam client settings without opening Steam.
  • Install, Remove, and Update Steam skins, and set the active skin.
